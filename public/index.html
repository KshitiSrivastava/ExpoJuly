OVERVIEW

Qatar aims to host an Expo that attracts the international community, including the participation of 80 countries, along with international organizations, private sector representatives, NGOs and the general public. Due to the anticipated diversity of participants, from top decision-makers to children, Expo 2023 Doha is inspired to offer a multifaceted event where extraordinary exhibitions, diplomatic encounters, business meetings, public debates and live shows will take place.

PROPOSED
THEMES

The Expo Doha 2023 is organized around the theme of ‘Desertification’ which is growing ecological issue across the globe.

The sub-themes of the event are Modern Agriculture, Technology & Innovation, Environmental Awareness & Sustainability.
